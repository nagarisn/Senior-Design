## Getting Started
### Creating an Account
1. Open the SmartTravel website and click on Sign Up.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Log in to access the dashboard.

### Navigating the Dashboard
- The dashboard provides a quick overview of your trip details, itinerary progress, and recent interactions with the travel chatbot.  
- Use the navigation options to access different features such as **Trip Planning**, **Chat with Travel Agent**, **Saved Itineraries**, and **Trip Preferences**.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### **Q: How do I start planning a trip using Smart Travel?**
```markdown
A: You can start by entering your destination and travel dates on the planning page or by chatting
   directly with the travel agent. The chatbot will guide you through the process and generate a
   personalized itinerary based on your inputs.
```

### **Q: Can I change my itinerary after it is generated?**
```markdown
A: Yes. You can interact with the chatbot to request changes such as adjusting the pace of the trip,
   modifying activities, or updating your travel preferences, and the itinerary will be
   updated accordingly. 
```
